Everyone should get the chance to hug an alpaca. They are gentle and sweet creatures with just a touch of sass. They can make wonderful pets (if you are skilled with a shear) and are beloved by many for their quirky personalities. The only thing better than an alpaca is a herd of alpacas. Though they may not look like the brightest of animals they are actually very intelligent. They respond brilliantly to clicker training and are able to learn a variety of tricks. All in all, they are just delightful animals who spread joy and happiness wherever they go.
